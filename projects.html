Warman Design and Build Competition

A competition designed to challenge students to push the boundaries of mechanical engineering.

The Warman Design and Build Competition is a national competition for engineering students to design and build a robot to complete a specific task. The goal of this project was to design a robot that could successfully navigate the course and deposit balls into designated areas, ultimately achieving a high score in the competition.
