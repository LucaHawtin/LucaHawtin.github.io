Background

Following my graduation from Shirley Boys' High School in 2023, I began pursuing my Bachelor of Engineering (Hons) in Mechanical Engineering at the University of Canterbury. Now in my third year and tracking toward a 2027 graduation, I am developing a broad mechanical foundation with a strong personal design interest in aerodynamics, lightweight structures, and the optimization of high-performance human-powered vehicles.

In my engineering work, I gravitate toward practical, hands-on problems where theoretical modeling meets physical execution, ranging from structural optimization and stress analysis to aerodynamic testing. I am highly comfortable moving between digital design and the workshop floor, utilizing tools like SolidWorks and Python for data analysis, alongside practical experience in machining, mechanical prototyping, and manufacture.

I am currently looking for a summer engineering internship starting in late 2026, where I can apply my grit, technical toolkit, and practical problem-solving skills to real-world mechanical engineering challenges.
